Section 02

Data We Collect

Account & Identity

  • Full name — to personalise your experience
  • Email address — for authentication and security notices
  • Password — stored as a secure hash; we never see your plaintext password
  • Age, weight, height — optionally provided during onboarding for personalised guidance

Health & Wellness

  • Menstrual cycle data — period dates, flow intensity, cycle length
  • Health conditions — self-reported (e.g. PCOS, thyroid, endometriosis, anxiety)
  • Symptom logs — energy, sleep, mood, physical and emotional symptoms
  • Habit tracking — movement, nutrition, stress, hydration records
  • Life stage — reproductive, postpartum, perimenopause, post-menopause

AI Conversation Data

  • Chat messages — messages you send to Aria and Aria's responses
  • Conversation history is stored locally on your device and sent to Anthropic's API only to generate a response

Technical Data

  • Device type and OS version (Firebase Analytics)
  • App usage events — screen views, feature interactions (no health content)
  • Firebase installation ID (anonymous device identifier)

What We Do Not Collect

  • Location data  ·  Contacts  ·  Camera or microphone  ·  Biometrics  ·  Financial information

Section 04

How Your Data Is Stored

On-Device Storage

Most health data — cycle logs, symptom entries, habits, and AI history — is stored locally on your device using encrypted device storage. It does not leave your device unless cloud sync is enabled.

Cloud Storage — Firebase

Account information and sync data are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) on Google Cloud Platform. Firebase complies with GDPR and encrypts data at rest and in transit. Our primary data centre is asia-south1 (Mumbai, India) supporting data residency preferences under India's DPDP Act 2023.

AI Processing — Anthropic

When you message Aria, your message and a contextual summary of your health data are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing in real time. This data is not used to train Anthropic's models under our API agreement.

Section 06

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only:

  • With service providers (Firebase, Anthropic) under strict data processing agreements
  • If required by law — court orders or government authority

Section 07

Sensitive Health Data

Bloom processes sensitive personal data under GDPR Article 9 (health data) and India's DPDP Act 2023. We process this data solely on the basis of your explicit, informed consent given during onboarding.

Safeguards we apply:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3 minimum)
  • Access restricted to authorised personnel under strict confidentiality obligations
  • No automated profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects
  • Regular security assessments of all third-party processors
Section 08

Your Rights — GDPR & DPDP Act

GDPR Rights (EU / UK Users)

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive data in machine-readable format (Settings → Download my data)
  • Restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

India DPDP Act 2023 Rights

  • Right to information — know what data is processed and why
  • Right of access — obtain a summary of your personal data
  • Right to correction and erasure — correct or delete your personal data
  • Right to grievance redressal — raise a complaint in-app or by email; we respond within timelines mandated by the DPDP Act
  • Right to nominate — nominate someone to exercise your rights in case of incapacity
To exercise any right: email privacy@bloomapp.com. We respond within 30 days.
Section 09

Data Deletion

In-App

  • Settings → Privacy & Data → Download my data (export first if desired)
  • Settings → Account → Delete Account — permanently deletes all data within 30 days

By Email

Email privacy@bloomapp.com with subject "Data Deletion Request" and your registered email. We confirm deletion within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account credentials and profile · All health logs, cycle data, symptoms, habits · AI conversation history · All Firebase records associated with your account
Note: Anonymised, aggregated analytics (which cannot identify you) may be retained for statistical purposes. Security audit logs are retained for 12 months as required by law.
